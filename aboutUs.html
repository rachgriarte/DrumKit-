THE COMPANY

NextUp L.L.C. was started by co-founders, Nicholas Donahue, Utshab Chakraborty, Wynne Plaga, with the goal of being able to provide a place for college entrepreneurs and startups to come together. We hope to be able to create an environment that helps startups thrive with mutual interactions and consulting.

ABOUT NEXTUP

NextUp Music is a Web and Phone based application that allows a you to queue music with the people around you similar to a digital jukebox. We currently allow the use of Spotify and Youtube and will be adding SoundCloud, Apple Music, Spinrilla, and your local music in the near future.


NextUp Music was started by three North Carolina State University and Universtiy of North Carolina students with the goal being able to connect users and music in every situation.
